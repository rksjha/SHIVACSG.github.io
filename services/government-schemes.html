Our 6-Step Process

  1. Eligibility Assessment — We evaluate your business profile against current scheme criteria and shortlist the best-fit schemes.
  2. DPR / Project Report Preparation — Our team prepares bankable Detailed Project Reports meeting all government and bank requirements.
  3. Application Filing — Complete form filling, documentation, and submission to the concerned government authority / KVIC / bank.
  4. Bank Co-ordination — Liaison with nationalised banks, SFBs, and NBFCs for loan sanction and scheme integration.
  5. Subsidy Claim Processing — Follow-up and claim filing with DFIBT / NABARD / state nodal agencies for disbursement.
  6. Compliance & Renewal — Annual compliance, utilisation certificate filing, and scheme renewal as required.
